Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a packet?

Tap the + button on the home screen. Fill in item details and tap Save.

How do I import a receipt PDF?

When adding or editing a packet, open the Receipt Photo section and tap Import Receipt PDF. Choose a PDF from Files.

How do I add a receipt or product photo?

In Add or Edit, use Scan Receipt / Add Photo for receipts or Add Product Photo for product images.

How do I export a proof packet?

Open a packet, tap Export PDF, then share or save the generated PDF.

How do the free limit and lifetime unlock work?

You can create up to 5 proof packets for free. To create more than 5 packets, unlock unlimited packets with a one-time lifetime purchase. If already purchased, tap Restore Purchase in Settings.

How do I set reminders?

In Dates and Deadlines, enable return or warranty reminders and choose your dates. Notifications are used only if you allow permission.

Does the app require an account?

No. ClaimReady Packets works entirely on your device without account login.
